ou l'ennui de la vue?

inspired by: John Berger's Ways of Seeing

by: halenur komsul

when does it ache the most? when you're looking or when you're not? because you weep in awe when you see the world's glory but wail in fear when you're missing it.

when does the noise get loudest? when you're listening or when you're not? you want to understand but you need to drown it out
